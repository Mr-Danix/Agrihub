AgriHub

How it works

1. Farmers Sign Up
Create account, add products with quantity, price, harvest date and location.
2. Buyers Browse
Filter by category or region; view price, available qty, and delivery options.
3. Place Orders
Choose delivery or pickup; add items to cart and checkout.
4. Payment
Pay with Telebirr, CBE Birr, other emoney, or cash on delivery.
5. Delivery
Farmers or local couriers deliver.
6. Feedback
Buyers rate farmers and products; farmers get improvement tips.
